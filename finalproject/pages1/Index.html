SEA MOSS

What is SEA MOSS?

Sea Moss, also known as Irish moss, is a seaweed that comes in many colors and is often used in fortified foods. It is one of the essential minerals like calcium and iodine, which are important for building strong bones and maintaining muscle mass. In addition, seaweed is rich in vitamins B, C and D and contains a large amount of fiber, which helps keep you comfortable and promote health.

Where does it come from?

Sea Moss is often harvested from the rocky Atlantic coasts of Europe and North America. Nori grows mostly in warm waters around Central and South America, the Caribbean, Africa, and Asia. SeaMoss is also known as Irish seaweed or Jamaican purple seaweed. There is evidence that humans have used spiny sea plants for over 14,000 years.
